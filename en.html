Our Services

Complete expertise for maintaining and repairing your vehicle

📅 All our services are provided by appointment only

Oil Change & Maintenance

Engine oil change, filter replacement (oil, air, cabin, fuel). Regular maintenance as per manufacturer recommendations to extend your vehicle's lifespan.

Brakes

Replacement of brake pads and discs, circuit bleeding, calliper inspection. Effective braking is essential for your safety.

Timing Belt

Replacement of the timing kit (belt or chain, tensioners, water pump). A crucial job to prevent engine damage.

Clutch

Full clutch kit replacement (disc, pressure plate, release bearing). Accurate diagnosis for slipping or gear selection issues.

Shock Absorbers & Suspension

Replacement of shock absorbers, springs, ball joints, tie rods and silent blocks. Restore comfort and road holding.

Tyres

Fitting, balancing and replacement of all tyre brands. Personalised advice for choosing your summer/winter tyres.

Electronic Diagnostics

Reading and clearing fault codes, complete engine diagnostics, warning light reset. Professional latest-generation equipment.

Pre-MOT Inspection

Complete check of all MOT test points. Avoid failure thanks to our detailed preventive inspection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit

Yes, we provide a free and detailed quote before any work is carried out. No work is done without your prior approval.

We work on all vehicle brands: Renault, Peugeot, Citroën, Volkswagen, BMW, Mercedes, Audi, Toyota, and many more.

Yes, all our services are provided by appointment only to ensure quality service and optimal turnaround times.
