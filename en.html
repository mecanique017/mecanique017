Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit

Yes, we provide a free and detailed quote before any work is carried out. No work is done without your prior approval.

We work on all vehicle brands: Renault, Peugeot, Citroën, Volkswagen, BMW, Mercedes, Audi, Toyota, and many more.

Yes, all our services are provided by appointment only to ensure quality service and optimal turnaround times.

Turnaround times vary depending on the type of work. For routine maintenance, allow 1 to 2 days. For more complex repairs, we will inform you of the exact timeframe when providing the quote.

We accept payment by credit card, cash and bank transfer. Payment in instalments may be arranged depending on the amount of the work.

We do not have courtesy vehicles available, but we can drop you off or arrange to pick up your vehicle subject to availability.
